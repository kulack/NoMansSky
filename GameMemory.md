## Recipies

* Nanite Clusters can be made from a variety of ingredients
  * 1 tainted metal makes 1 Nanite Cluster
  * 5 Mold makes makes 1 Nanite Cluster
  * 1 Larval core makes 50 Nanite clusters
  * 1 Haldal core makes 50 Nanite clusters
  * Atlantium makes nanite clusters (No amounts known yet)
  * Radieant Shard makes nanite clusters (No amounts known yet)

* Mold (Mold or Mould or runaway mold)
  * 1 Living slime makes 1 mold

## Stars/Systems

* Text description of a star in the galaxy map indicates it's color based on the prefix of the description.
 * F or G (Yellow): Common, no special drive needed.
 * K or M (Red/Orange): Requires Cadmium Drive.
 * E (Green): Requires Emeril Drive.
 * B or O (Blue): Requires Indium Drive.
 * X or Y (Purple): Requires Atlantid Drive.
